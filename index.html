About Declare Family

Declare Family is a vibrant, youth-driven community committed to empowering young people to grow, lead, and create meaningful impact across every dimension of life.

We bring together passionate individuals determined to pursue growth — spiritually, intellectually, socially, psychologically, and economically.

At the heart of Declare Family is the belief that young people thrive best within a supportive ecosystem where learning, collaboration, mentorship, and service intersect.

Declare Family is more than a gathering — it is a network of emerging leaders and change-makers committed to personal excellence and collective advancement.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To cultivate a generation of visionary and purpose-driven young leaders empowered to positively influence society and contribute meaningfully to the advancement of their communities and the world.

Our Mission

To identify, nurture, and empower young people by providing an environment that fosters holistic growth, critical thinking, leadership development, and genuine community engagement.

Our Core Values

Growth

Continuous intellectual, spiritual, and personal development as a lifelong pursuit.

Unity

A culture of belonging, collaboration, and mutual support among all members.

Leadership

Initiative, responsibility, and visionary thinking at every level of the community.

Integrity

Honesty, accountability, and ethical character in all our dealings and commitments.

Impact

Meaningful, lasting change within our communities and the world around us.

Our Programs & Activities

Declare Family operates through carefully structured programs designed to inspire growth, dialogue, and meaningful engagement among young people.

Scriptural Insight

Daily Wisdom Capsules

Civic Support Projects

Collaborative initiatives focused on community needs and active volunteerism.

Faith Engagement

Financial Intelligence

Conversations on financial literacy, entrepreneurship, and sustainable wealth creation.
